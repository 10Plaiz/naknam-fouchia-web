MONCLER

Logo-Appliquéd Striped Cotton-Piqué Polo Shirt
£295

The Moncler Logo-Appliquéd Striped Cotton-Piqué Polo Shirt is a refined take on a sporty classic, combining the brand’s signature elegance with casual versatility. Crafted from breathable cotton-piqué, this polo offers a lightweight and comfortable feel, making it an essential piece for everyday wear. The design features bold yet sophisticated stripes, adding a dynamic touch to its timeless silhouette. A ribbed collar and cuffs provide a structured finish, while the two-button placket allows for adjustable styling. The iconic Moncler logo appliqué on the chest serves as a subtle yet distinctive nod to the brand’s heritage. Whether paired with tailored shorts, denim, or casual trousers, this polo shirt delivers effortless sophistication with a modern, athletic edge.

LOEWE

Ballet Runner 2.0 Logo-Appliquéd Shell, Suede and Leather Sneakers
£661

The Loewe Ballet Runner 2.0 Logo-Appliquéd Shell, Suede, and Leather Sneakers offer a unique fusion of vintage-inspired aesthetics and modern craftsmanship. Expertly crafted from a mix of shell, suede, and smooth leather, these sneakers showcase a rich interplay of textures that enhance their refined yet sporty appeal. The streamlined silhouette takes cues from classic running shoes, with a lightweight construction that ensures all-day comfort. The signature Loewe logo appliqué on the side adds a bold, distinctive touch, while the sculpted rubber sole provides excellent grip and durability. Designed for both style and function, these sneakers effortlessly complement casual and elevated outfits alike, making them a versatile staple in any contemporary wardrobe.

BRUNELLO CUCINELLI

Straight-Leg Cotton-Blend Jersey Drawstring Shorts
£981

The Brunello Cucinelli Straight-Leg Cotton-Blend Jersey Drawstring Shorts embody the brand’s signature blend of luxury and casual sophistication. Crafted in Italy from a premium cotton-blend jersey, these shorts offer a soft, lightweight feel with just the right amount of stretch for optimal comfort. The straight-leg cut creates a clean, tailored silhouette, while the adjustable drawstring waistband ensures a customizable fit. Thoughtful details such as tonal stitching, discreet side pockets, and subtle branding reflect Brunello Cucinelli’s refined approach to everyday essentials. Perfect for relaxed weekends, travel, or warm-weather layering, these versatile shorts pair effortlessly with everything from linen shirts to casual knitwear, delivering an elevated take on laid-back dressing.

ANITA KO

Zoe 18-Karat Gold Diamond Bracelet
£24,525

The Anita Ko Zoe 18-Karat Gold Diamond Bracelet is a timeless expression of understated luxury and elegance. Expertly crafted from polished 18-karat gold, this delicate yet striking piece features a refined chain adorned with dazzling, meticulously set diamonds that catch the light with every movement. Designed for effortless wear, its lightweight construction and secure clasp ensure a comfortable fit, making it perfect for both everyday elegance and special occasions. Whether worn alone for a subtle statement or layered with other fine jewelry, this bracelet embodies Anita Ko’s modern yet timeless approach to luxury, offering a sophisticated touch to any ensemble.
